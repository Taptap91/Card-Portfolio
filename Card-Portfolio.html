CardMenuA new way to manage loyalty cards and manage subscriptions
DesignerWriterResearcher
05/06 - 12/07A loyalty card app can be a great way to organize ones loyalty cards, or an extra frustration. Card is designed to make the process easy from signing up for loyalty cards, accessing your cards and managing your email subscriptionsResumeBackgroundLoyalty card apps are going to be a fixture in the digital world where every company is vying for your patronage. Keeping your cards in one place and managing your subscriptions and points should be centralized and that is the purpose of the Card app
The Problem
Signing up for retail emails and savings is irritating and managing your email subscriptions means doing it one at a time
The GoalThe app is designed to speed up the sign up and opt out process as well as keep member cards all in place
The Opportunity
Users want to be able to sign up for loyalty programs to save money, but want to do it quicklyOpting out of email subscriptions needs to be available at any moment, not when the email arrives or using questionable third party services
